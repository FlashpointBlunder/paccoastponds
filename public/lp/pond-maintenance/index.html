What's Included Every Visit

Every service visit covers the essentials — and we document everything in a report sent directly to your inbox after we leave.

🧪

Water Chemistry Testing

pH, ammonia, nitrite, nitrate, KH, and salt tested every visit. Treatments applied as needed to keep your koi in the ideal range.

⚙️

Filter & Equipment Service

Skimmer cleaning, filter media rinse, pump inspection, and UV clarifier check. We catch problems before they become emergencies.

🌿

Algae Control & Debris Removal

String algae removal, surface debris net, pond wall brush-down. We treat the cause — not just the symptom.

Fish Health Monitoring

Visual health assessment every visit — behavior, color, fin condition. Early detection of disease or stress before it becomes critical.
