How much does a koi pond cost in Orange County?

Most backyard koi pond projects in Orange County start around $25,000 and scale based on size, depth, filtration, and design features. We provide free 3D renderings and fixed-price proposals.

Do you offer free koi pond design renderings?

Yes! Every project includes a complimentary 3D rendering so you can visualize your pond design before construction begins.

Which areas of Southern California do you serve?

We design and build ponds throughout Orange County, Los Angeles County, San Diego County, and Riverside County.

How long does koi pond construction take?

Most pond builds take 2–4 weeks depending on size, complexity, and permitting requirements. We handle project management from start to finish.

Can you remodel or upgrade an existing pond?

Absolutely. We specialize in pond remodels, filtration upgrades, and transforming outdated water features into modern koi-friendly systems.

Will a koi pond increase my home’s value?

Yes, a professionally designed koi pond or water feature can increase property value and curb appeal. Many buyers see it as a luxury landscaping upgrade similar to a pool or outdoor kitchen.

Are koi ponds high maintenance?

Not when they’re built correctly. Our systems use bottom drains, skimmers, and professional-grade filtration to keep water clear with minimal work. Most clients spend less than 15 minutes a week on maintenance.

Do koi ponds attract mosquitos?

No. A properly circulating pond with koi fish and filtration does not allow stagnant water where mosquitos breed. In fact, koi often eat mosquito larvae.

What kind of space do I need for a pond?

We can design ponds to fit nearly any yard. While larger spaces allow for deeper, multi-feature ponds, even small backyards can host a modern pondless waterfall or compact koi pond.

Is a pond safe for children and pets?

Yes, with the right design. We can add shallow zones, safety edging, and pondless systems (where water disappears underground) for families with small children or pets.

What kind of warranty do you provide?

All pond construction includes a 5-year workmanship warranty. Equipment such as pumps and filters carry manufacturer warranties for additional peace of mind.

Do I need a permit to build a pond?

In most residential areas, small to medium ponds do not require permits. For larger projects or structural builds, we’ll advise if permits are necessary and help guide you through the process.
