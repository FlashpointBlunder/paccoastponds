Do koi ponds attract mosquitos?

No. A properly circulating pond with koi fish and filtration does not allow stagnant water where mosquitos breed. In fact, koi often eat mosquito larvae.

What kind of space do I need for a pond?

We can design ponds to fit nearly any yard. While larger spaces allow for deeper, multi-feature ponds, even small backyards can host a modern pondless waterfall or compact koi pond.

Is a pond safe for children and pets?

Yes, with the right design. We can add shallow zones, safety edging, and pondless systems (where water disappears underground) for families with small children or pets.
