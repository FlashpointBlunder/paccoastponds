Do you serve Anaheim Hills?

Yes — Anaheim Hills is a core part of our North Orange County service area. We build in Canyon Rim, The Summit, Anaheim Hills Estates, and throughout Anaheim Hills' hillside and planned communities.

What's the typical koi pond cost in Anaheim Hills?

Most koi pond projects in Anaheim Hills start around $25,000 for a well-built, professionally filtered pond. Hillside builds, natural rock work, and larger volumes scale from there. We provide free 3D renderings and fixed-price proposals so you know the exact cost before we break ground.

Are koi ponds high maintenance?

Not when they're built correctly. Our systems use bottom drains, skimmers, and professional-grade filtration to keep water clear with minimal work. Most clients spend less than 15 minutes a week on maintenance.

How long does koi pond construction take?

Most pond builds take 2–4 weeks depending on size, complexity, and permitting requirements. We handle project management from start to finish.
